Help & FAQ

1. How does the Muscle Recovery model work?
It uses your workout duration, intensity, age, height, weight, daily protein, and sleep to calculate a precise recovery time using an advanced machine learning algorithm.

2. How are Calories and Macros predicted?
Fill out your body metrics and fitness goals. The AI calculates your BMR and TDEE, then splits it into optimal macros (Protein, Carbs, Fats) depending on if you want to lose, gain, or maintain weight.

3. Why should I fill out my Profile?
Your Age, Gender, Height, and Weight in the settings menu will automatically pre-fill all prediction forms to save you time.
